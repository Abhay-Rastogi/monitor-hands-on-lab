# <u> Download Maximo Visual Inspection Mobile </u>

1. Go to the App Store. (Only available on iOS 13 or later)

            

2. Type <b>IBM Maximo Visual Inspection</b> in the search bar.

    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/applogo.png){: style="height:190px;width:210px;margin-left:40px"}



3. Download the app. 



4. Open the app and click on each of the buttons in <span style="color:red">red.</span> 



    - <b>Terms and Conditions:</b>

    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/termsNconditions.jpeg){: style="height:400px;width:250px;margin-left:40px"}

    - <b>Allow MVI to access your camera, bluetooth, and photos, </b> Select <b>I already have an account:</b>

    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/cameraA.png){: style="height:400px;width:250px;margin-left:40px"}
    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/bluetoothA.png){: style="height:400px;width:250px;margin-left:40px"}
    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/photoAccess.png){: style="height:400px;width:250px;margin-left:40px"}

    - Enter your information in the <b>Name</b> and <b>Location</b> fields.

    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/nameLocation.jpeg){: style="height:300px;width:250px;margin-left:40px"}

5. Select the settings symbol ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/setting.jpg){: style="height:30px;width:30px"} in the top left corner of your screen. Have <b>Handheld Mode</b> switched on and upload your credentials. 
    - <b>username:</b> See instructor.
    - <b>password:</b> See instructor.
    - <b>Base URL:</b> https://mvi-test.hccx.io/visual-inspection/api - See instructor for your base url just make sure <b>/api</b> is at the end.

    ![img](/img/mvi-hol-imgs/img-prereqs/loginSettings.png){: style="height:400px;width:250px;margin-left:40px"}
    

    (Make sure there are no typos in url or you won't be able to connect to the server.)


6. Click <b>Save</b>.

    <h1>You are ready to <u>begin!</u></h1>
